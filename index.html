About unifa.lauch

unifa.lauch is a fair token launch platform powered by the 402 protocol, designed to address the issues of unfair token distribution and project abandonment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a fair and transparent environment for token launches, where investors can participate on equal terms and project developers are held accountable for delivering on their promises.

We aim to revolutionize the token launch space by implementing the 402 protocol, which ensures that funds are allocated appropriately and development progress is monitored transparently.

How It Works

Discover the simple steps to launch or invest in tokens on our fair and transparent platform.

1. Project Application

Developers submit their project for review, including a detailed roadmap, tokenomics, and smart contract code. Our team conducts a thorough verification process.

2. Launch Preparation

Approved projects set a launch date, hard cap, and price. The smart contract is deployed with built-in milestone tracking and fund release mechanisms.

3. Fair Token Sale

Investors participate at the same price during the token sale period. All contributions are held in a secure escrow contract until the hard cap is reached.

4. Fund Allocation

When the hard cap is reached, funds are automatically split: 40% to liquidity pool and 60% to development fund with milestone-based release.

5. Progress Monitoring

The community monitors development progress against predefined milestones through transparent reporting. Funds are released to developers only when milestones are verified by community vote.

6. Ongoing Governance

Token holders participate in DAO governance to vote on project improvements, additional funding, and other key decisions.

When a token sale reaches its hard cap, funds are automatically allocated as follows:

  • 40% to Liquidity Pool: Immediately added to decentralized exchanges to enable trading.
  • 60% to Development Fund: Controlled by smart contract, released to developers based on verified progress and community voting.

Fund Release Mechanism

The 60% development fund is controlled by a smart contract. Project teams can apply for fund releases based on their development progress. The community votes to approve or deny these requests, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Community Governance

Our platform is governed by a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), where token holders can propose and vote on platform improvements, project listings, and other key decisions.

Proposal Submission

Token holders can submit proposals for platform improvements, new features, or changes to existing policies.

Discussion Period

Proposals undergo a community discussion period where members can provide feedback and suggest modifications.

Voting Process

Token holders vote on proposals using their governance tokens. The voting power is proportional to the number of tokens held.

Implementation

Approved proposals are implemented by the development team and monitored by the community.
