unifa.launch is a decentralized platform that enables fair, transparent, and community-governed token launches. Our innovative mechanism ensures equal opportunities for all participants while protecting against rug pulls.

Why Choose unifa.launch?

Our platform combines the simplicity of Pump.fun with advanced features that ensure fairness, transparency, and community governance.

Fair Launch Mechanism

No pre-sales, no team allocations. Every participant gets equal access at the same price through our transparent bonding curve.

Anti-Rug Pull Protection

40% of funds go to liquidity pool, 60% to development fund controlled by smart contract and released based on milestones.

Development Progress Tracking

Public milestones and progress tracking ensure transparency and accountability from project teams.

DAO Governance

Community-driven decision making through our DAO governance system. Token holders vote on key proposals and project direction.

Transparent Fund Allocation

Real-time visualization of fund distribution between liquidity pool and development fund.

No-Code Token Creation

Launch your token in minutes with our intuitive interface. No coding skills required.
