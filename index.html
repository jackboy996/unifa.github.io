Launch Guide

Creator Process

  1. Connect Phantom Wallet
  2. Fill in token parameters (name, symbol, total supply, price, etc.)
  3. Set target amount and reserve ratio (10%-50%)
  4. Submit form and pay a small amount of SOL as gas fee
  5. Wait for investors to purchase your tokens
  6. Automatically create Raydium LP after reaching the target (within 7 days)
  7. Automatic refund to investors if target not reached

Investor Process

  1. Connect Phantom Wallet
  2. Browse available token list
  3. Select the token you are interested in
  4. Click the "Buy" button
  5. Choose payment method (SOL or USDC)
  6. Confirm transaction and pay
  7. Receive tokens and trade on Raydium after target is reached
  8. Automatically receive refund if target not reached

Key Rules

  • Launch period is 7 days
  • Success condition: Raised amount meets or exceeds target amount
  • LP creation ratio: 100% of raised funds + reserved tokens
  • Refund timing: Automatic refund within 24 hours after expiration
  • Payment methods: SOL and USDC-SPL supported
  • Token standard: SPL token (8 decimal places)
  • All transactions occur on the Solana blockchain, ensuring transparency and traceability

Fee Structure

  • Creator token creation: Small SOL gas fee required
  • Investor token purchase: No additional fees, only token price
  • Platform does not charge any fees

Security Tips

  • Ensure you use this platform in a secure network environment
  • Carefully verify token information to avoid purchasing counterfeit tokens
  • Only operate on official websites and be vigilant against phishing sites
  • Investment involves risks, please be cautious when entering the market
